Welcome to the Cat Adoption!

Who Are We?

The Cat Adoption a local adoption center founded in 2010. We are made possible through our lovely volunteers, charity events, and the generous donations that we receive.

The Cat Adoption was founded to help as many cats as possible find their forever home.

Every year, according to the ASPCA, 1.4 million cats are euthanized. We want to make a difference in those numbers, no matter the size. Every cat is important to us. We appreciate each and every one of our volunteers.

If you are looking to volunteer today, please contact us via our About section.

Our Goal

Our main goal is to help every furry cat friend find their perfect fit home. If you are interested in adopting a furry friend, please visit us in person. We will be happy to help you meet the cat of your choice in person, and help you fill out an adoption interest form.

Thank you for supporting The Cat Adoption!

Charity Events

The Cat Adoption hosts multiple charity events a year. If you are interested in being a volunteer, please visit our Volunteer page or visit us in person, today!
